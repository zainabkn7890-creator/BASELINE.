For Every Learner

Designed Around Accessibility

  • Atkinson Hyperlegible font — optimized for low-vision readers
  • Word-by-word highlight synced with text-to-speech
  • Syllable breakdown mode for complex words
  • Background color presets to reduce visual stress
  • Adjustable letter spacing and line height
  • Focus line mode — one line at a time
  • WCAG AA+ contrast throughout
  • Reduce motion toggle for vestibular sensitivity
